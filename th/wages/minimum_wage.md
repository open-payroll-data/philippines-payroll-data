## Summary
Thailand's minimum wage is set **per province as a daily rate** by the Wage Committee. Under **Notification No. 13** (effective **1 January 2025**), the rate ranges from **฿337 to ฿400 per day** across 17 tiers. The **฿400** top rate applies in **Chachoengsao, Chon Buri, Phuket, Rayong, and Koh Samui district (Surat Thani)**; Bangkok and its surrounding provinces are **฿372**; the lowest **฿337** applies in Narathiwat, Pattani, and Yala.

> Currency: **฿ / THB**. Set by the **Wage Committee** under the Ministry of Labour.

> `volatility: continuously-changing` — provincial daily rates are revised by Wage Committee notification roughly annually (sometimes with mid-year sector/area-specific changes). **Re-pull the latest notification before relying on a rate.**
