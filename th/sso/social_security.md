## Summary
Thailand's **Social Security Fund (SSF)** is **5% employee + 5% employer**, on a monthly wage **floored at ฿1,650** and **capped at ฿17,500** — so the contribution ranges **฿82.50–฿875/month each side** (max **฿875**). **⚠ The ceiling rose from ฿15,000 (max ฿750) to ฿17,500 (max ฿875) on 1 January 2026** (enacted by Royal Gazette, 12 Dec 2025), and phases up further to ฿20,000 (2029) and ฿23,000 (2032). Separately, the **Workmen's Compensation Fund (WCF)** is **employer-only**, **0.2%–1.0%** of annual wages by industry risk, capped at **฿240,000/year per employee**. Remit both by the **15th of the following month** (Form **SSO 1-10**).

> Currency: **฿ / THB**. Administrator: **Social Security Office (SSO)** (สำนักงานประกันสังคม). SSF applies to Section 33 employees.

## SSF contribution (Section 33)
| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| Rate — employee | **5%** |
| Rate — employer | **5%** |
| Wage floor | **฿1,650/month** |
| Wage ceiling (from 1 Jan 2026) | **฿17,500/month** |
| Max contribution each side (from 1 Jan 2026) | **฿875/month** |
| Min contribution each side | ฿82.50/month |

### Wage-ceiling schedule (enacted — Royal Gazette 12 Dec 2025)
| Period | Floor | Ceiling | Max each side (5%) |
|--------|-------|---------|--------------------|
| Pre-2026 (historical) | ฿1,650 | ฿15,000 | ฿750 |
| **1 Jan 2026 – 31 Dec 2028** | ฿1,650 | **฿17,500** | **฿875** |
| 1 Jan 2029 – 31 Dec 2031 | ฿1,650 | ฿20,000 | ฿1,000 |
| From 1 Jan 2032 | ฿1,650 | ฿23,000 | ฿1,150 |

The SSF covers 7 benefit categories: sickness/injury, maternity, invalidity, death, child allowance, old-age pension, unemployment.

## Workmen's Compensation Fund (WCF)
| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| Who pays | **Employer only** |
| Rate | **0.2% – 1.0%** of annual wages (by industry/business risk category; from year 5 adjusted by the firm's own accident experience) |
| Wage cap | **฿240,000/year per employee** |

## Remittance
- **Deadline: by the 15th of the following month.** Form **SSO 1-10 (สปส.1-10)**. Late penalty **2% per month** on the shortfall.

---

## Quick example (employee, monthly wage ฿30,000, 2026)
Wage above the ฿17,500 ceiling → SSF on ฿17,500: **employee ฿875 (5%)** deducted, **employer ฿875**. (Before 2026 this was ฿750 each.)
