## Summary
On dismissal without cause (and not for serious misconduct), an employee with **≥ 120 days** of continuous service is entitled to statutory **severance pay** scaled by tenure — from **30 days' wages** (120 days–<1 year) up to **400 days' wages** (≥ 20 years). The top **400-day** tier was added by the **2019 amendment** (LPA No. 7 B.E. 2562).

> Currency: **฿ / THB**. Statute: Labour Protection Act B.E. 2541 §118, as amended 2019.

## Severance pay schedule (§118, current)
| Continuous service | Severance (days of the last wage rate) |
|--------------------|:--------------------------------------:|
| 120 days – < 1 year | **30 days** |
| 1 – < 3 years | **90 days** |
| 3 – < 6 years | **180 days** |
| 6 – < 10 years | **240 days** |
| 10 – < 20 years | **300 days** |
| ≥ 20 years | **400 days** |

- Payable on dismissal without cause; **not** payable for the serious-misconduct grounds in §119 (e.g. dishonesty, intentional damage, gross negligence).
- Separate from pay-in-lieu of advance notice and from any special redundancy pay for relocation/machinery-replacement cases.
