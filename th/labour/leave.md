## Summary
Under the **Labour Protection Act**, employees get **≥ 6 days** paid annual leave after 1 year of service, up to **30 days** paid sick leave per year, and **≥ 13 public holidays** per year. **⚠ Maternity leave rose from 98 to 120 days on 7 December 2025** (LPA No. 9 B.E. 2568): the employer pays full wages for up to **60 days** and the Social Security Fund pays a portion of the rest. The 2025 amendment also adds **15 days' paternity/spousal leave** (full pay) and **15 days' childcare leave** (50%).

> Currency: **฿ / THB**. Statute: Labour Protection Act B.E. 2541, as amended by **LPA No. 9 B.E. 2568 (2025)**.

## Annual leave (§30)
**≥ 6 working days/year** after **1 year** of continuous service (pro-rated for < 1 year by agreement).

## Sick leave (§32 / §57)
Paid sick leave for as long as actually sick, **up to 30 working days/year** at full pay. A medical certificate may be required for **3 or more** days.

## Public holidays (§29)
**≥ 13 traditional public holidays/year**, announced in advance, **including National Labour Day**.

## Maternity & family leave (LPA No. 9 B.E. 2568, in force 7 Dec 2025)
| Leave | Current (from 7 Dec 2025) | Pre-2025 (until 6 Dec 2025) |
|-------|---------------------------|------------------------------|
| Maternity | **120 days** | 98 days |
| — employer-paid portion | up to **60 days** at full wage | 45 days |
| — Social Security portion | remaining ~60 days at 50% (SSF, capped ฿17,500/mo) *(interpreted)* | 45 days at 50% |
| Paternity / spousal leave | **15 days** (full pay) — new | — |
| Childcare leave | **15 days** (50%) — new | — |
