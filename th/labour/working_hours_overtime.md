## Summary
Under the **Labour Protection Act B.E. 2541**, normal working hours are **≤ 8 hours/day and ≤ 48 hours/week**, with a rest period of **≥ 1 hour** after no more than 5 consecutive hours and a **weekly holiday of ≥ 1 day**. **Overtime** on a normal day is **1.5×** the hourly rate; **work on a holiday** is paid at an additional **1× (2× total)** for those entitled to holiday pay (or 2× for those not); **overtime on a holiday is 3×**.

> Currency: **฿ / THB**. Statute: **Labour Protection Act B.E. 2541 (1998)**, administered by the Ministry of Labour / DLPW.

## Working hours & rest
| Item | Rule | Section |
|------|------|---------|
| Normal hours | ≤ **8 hours/day**, ≤ **48 hours/week** | §23 |
| Hazardous work | ≤ 7 hours/day, ≤ 42 hours/week (set by Ministerial Regulation) | §23 + reg. |
| Rest period | ≥ **1 hour** after ≤ 5 consecutive hours of work | §27 |
| Weekly holiday | ≥ **1 day/week** (interval ≤ 6 days) | §28 |

## Overtime & holiday pay multipliers
| Scenario | Multiplier | Section |
|----------|:----------:|---------|
| Overtime on a normal working day | **1.5×** the hourly rate | §61 |
| Work on a holiday — employee **entitled** to holiday pay (e.g. monthly-paid) | **+1× = 2× total** | §62(1) |
| Work on a holiday — employee **not entitled** to holiday pay (e.g. daily-paid) | **2×** | §62(2) |
| Overtime on a holiday | **3×** the hourly rate | §63 |
