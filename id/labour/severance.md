## Summary
On termination, Indonesian employees may be entitled to three components under **PP 35/2021**: **uang pesangon** (severance, **1×–9× monthly wage** by tenure), **uang penghargaan masa kerja** (long-service reward, **2×–10×** by tenure), and **uang penggantian hak** (compensation of rights). The **base tables** below are then multiplied by a **reason-specific factor** (e.g. 0.5×, 1×, or 2× the pesangon) depending on the ground for termination.

> Currency: **Rp / IDR**. Regulation: **PP 35/2021** (implementing UU Cipta Kerja). Wage base = basic + fixed allowance.

## Uang Pesangon — severance (Pasal 40(2))
| Years of service | Severance (months' wage) |
|------------------|:------------------------:|
| < 1 | 1 |
| 1 – < 2 | 2 |
| 2 – < 3 | 3 |
| 3 – < 4 | 4 |
| 4 – < 5 | 5 |
| 5 – < 6 | 6 |
| 6 – < 7 | 7 |
| 7 – < 8 | 8 |
| **≥ 8** | **9** (cap) |

## Uang Penghargaan Masa Kerja — long-service reward (Pasal 40(3))
| Years of service | Reward (months' wage) |
|------------------|:---------------------:|
| 3 – < 6 | 2 |
| 6 – < 9 | 3 |
| 9 – < 12 | 4 |
| 12 – < 15 | 5 |
| 15 – < 18 | 6 |
| 18 – < 21 | 7 |
| 21 – < 24 | 8 |
| **≥ 24** | **10** |

## Uang Penggantian Hak (Pasal 40(4))
Untaken/un-expired annual leave + repatriation costs for the worker and family + other items set in the contract / company regulation / CBA.

> **Reason multiplier:** the pesangon payable is the base table above × a factor set by the termination ground — e.g. **2× pesangon** for death, retirement, or permanent disability; **1×** or **0.5×** for various efficiency/other grounds (PP 35/2021 Pasal 41–57). Long-service reward is generally **1×** the table.
