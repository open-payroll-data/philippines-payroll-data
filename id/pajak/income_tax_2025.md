## Summary
Indonesia taxes employment income via **PPh Pasal 21**. Annual taxable income (PKP) is taxed on a **progressive 5%–35%** scale (UU HPP, top rate **35%** above **Rp5 billion**). Each employee has a **PTKP** (non-taxable threshold, from **Rp54,000,000** for a single person). Since **1 January 2024** (PP 58/2023), employers withhold **monthly PPh 21 (Jan–Nov) using the TER (average effective rate) tables** by PTKP category (**A/B/C**), then do the **full annual progressive calculation in December** and reconcile. Deposit the withheld tax by the **15th** of the following month and file the monthly SPT Masa by the **20th**; give each employee an annual **Bukti Potong 1721-A1**.

> Currency: Indonesian Rupiah (**Rp / IDR**). Tax authority: **DJP** (Direktorat Jenderal Pajak). Tax year = calendar year.

## Annual progressive PPh 21 rates (UU HPP, from tax year 2022)
| Annual taxable income (PKP) | Rate |
|-----------------------------|:----:|
| 0 – Rp60,000,000 | 5% |
| > Rp60,000,000 – Rp250,000,000 | 15% |
| > Rp250,000,000 – Rp500,000,000 | 25% |
| > Rp500,000,000 – Rp5,000,000,000 | 30% |
| > Rp5,000,000,000 | **35%** |

## PTKP — non-taxable income (Penghasilan Tidak Kena Pajak)
Base **Rp54,000,000** (single); **+Rp4,500,000** if married; **+Rp4,500,000** per dependent (max 3).

| Status | PTKP (Rp) | Status | PTKP (Rp) |
|--------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| TK/0 | 54,000,000 | K/0 | 58,500,000 |
| TK/1 | 58,500,000 | K/1 | 63,000,000 |
| TK/2 | 63,000,000 | K/2 | 67,500,000 |
| TK/3 | 67,500,000 | K/3 | 72,000,000 |

**Taxable income (PKP)** = annual net income − PTKP. Net income = gross − **biaya jabatan** (5% of gross, capped **Rp500,000/month** = Rp6,000,000/year) − employee pension/JHT contributions.

## The TER monthly withholding method (from 1 Jan 2024)
PP 58/2023 + PMK 168/2023 changed how employers withhold **monthly**:
- **January–November:** withhold = **gross monthly income × the monthly TER rate** (TER Bulanan), read from the table for the employee's **TER category** and income level (rates range 0%–34%).
- **December (last month):** compute the **full-year liability** on the **annual progressive scale** (above) on PKP, then December withholding = annual liability − tax already withheld Jan–Nov. Over the full year the total equals the normal annual calculation.

**TER categories → PTKP status:**
| TER category | PTKP statuses |
|--------------|---------------|
| **A** | TK/0, TK/1, K/0 |
| **B** | TK/2, TK/3, K/1, K/2 |
| **C** | K/3 |

*(A separate **daily** TER applies to casual/daily workers.)* For the exact monthly rate, use the official DJP TER tables — the full grids are not reproduced here (see caveats).

## Employer obligations
| Obligation | Deadline | Form |
|------------|----------|------|
| Deposit withheld PPh 21 | **15th** of the following month (PMK 81/2024) | billing/SSP |
| File monthly return | **20th** of the following month | SPT Masa PPh 21 (e-Bupot 21/26) |
| Annual withholding slip to each employee | within 1 month of year-end (or on leaving) | **Bukti Potong 1721-A1** |

An employee with a single employer for the full year receives one annual 1721-A1 and files the personal annual return (SPT Tahunan 1770 S / 1770 SS).
