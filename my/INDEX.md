## Key authorities & instruments

| Authority / Instrument | Covers |
|------------------------|--------|
| LHDN/IRBM (Income Tax Act 1967) | Income tax, MTD/PCB, employer returns (Form E/EA) |
| EPF/KWSP (EPF Act 1991) | Provident-fund contributions |
| PERKESO (Act 4 + Act 800) | SOCSO (injury/invalidity) + EIS (employment insurance) |
| HRD Corp (PSMB Act 2001) | Employer training levy |
| MOHR (Employment Act 1955, amended 2022) | Coverage, hours/overtime, leave, termination |
| Minimum Wages Order 2024, P.U.(A) 376 | National minimum wage |

> Scope note: the **Employment Act 1955** applies to **Peninsular Malaysia + Labuan**; **Sabah** (Labour Ordinance Cap. 67) and **Sarawak** (Cap. 76) have separate laws. Tax, EPF, SOCSO/EIS apply nationally.
