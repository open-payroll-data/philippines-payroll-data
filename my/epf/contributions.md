## Summary
**EPF (KWSP)** is Malaysia's mandatory retirement provident fund. For employees **below age 60**, the standard rates are **employee 11%** and **employer 13%** (monthly wages **≤ RM5,000**) or **12%** (wages **> RM5,000**). For wages up to **RM20,000** the contribution is read from the **Third Schedule wage bands** (not an exact %), with the total **rounded up to the next ringgit**; above RM20,000 the exact percentages apply. There is **no wage ceiling**. From **1 October 2025**, EPF became **mandatory for non-citizen employees** at **2% employer + 2% employee**.

> Currency: **RM**. Administrator: **KWSP / EPF** (Kumpulan Wang Simpanan Pekerja / Employees Provident Fund), under the **EPF Act 1991**.

## Standard rates — Malaysian citizens & PRs (below age 60)
| Monthly wage | Employee | Employer |
|--------------|:--------:|:--------:|
| **RM5,000 and below** | **11%** | **13%** |
| **More than RM5,000** | **11%** | **12%** |

- **Computation method:** for wages **up to RM20,000**, use the **Third Schedule** fixed-RM bands; the **total contribution is rounded up to the next ringgit**. For wages **above RM20,000**, apply the exact percentages.
- **No wage ceiling** — contributions continue on the full wage (citizens/PRs).

## Age 60 and above
| Member | Employee | Employer |
|--------|:--------:|:--------:|
| Malaysian citizen, 60+ | **0%** | **4%** |
| PR / non-Malaysian (member before 1 Aug 1998), 60+ | 5.5% | 6.5% (≤RM5,000) / 6% (>RM5,000) |

## Non-citizen employees — mandatory from 1 October 2025
- **Rate: 2% employer + 2% employee** (Third Schedule **Part F**), for both below-60 and 60+.
- **Effective:** **October 2025 wages → November 2025 contribution month** (payable by 15 Nov 2025).
- **Who:** non-Malaysian employees under 75 with a valid passport + work pass; **excludes domestic servants** (maids, cooks, gardeners, drivers, etc.).
- Note: Malaysian **PRs are not** in the 2% tier — they remain on the standard 11%/13%/12% rates.
- Non-citizen EPF must be paid in **whole ringgit** (no cents).

## Quick example (citizen, below 60, wage RM4,000)
- Wage ≤ RM5,000 → employer 13%, employee 11%.
- Indicative: employer ≈ RM520 (13%), employee ≈ RM440 (11%) — but use the **Third Schedule band** for the exact rounded figure (wage ≤ RM20,000).
