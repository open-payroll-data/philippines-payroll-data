## Summary
Under the **Employment Act 1955** (as amended 2022), EA-covered employees get paid **annual leave of 8 / 12 / 16 days** (by years of service), **sick leave of 14 / 18 / 22 days** outpatient (plus up to **60 days** hospitalisation), and at least **11 gazetted public holidays** (5 compulsory). The 2022 amendment raised **maternity leave to 98 consecutive days** and introduced **7 days' paternity leave**.

> Currency: **RM**. Scope: Peninsular Malaysia + Labuan (Sabah/Sarawak have separate ordinances). Most entitlements require ≥ 12 months' or a defined qualifying service.

## Annual leave (s.60E)
| Continuous service | Paid annual leave / 12 months |
|--------------------|:-----------------------------:|
| < 2 years | **8 days** |
| 2 to < 5 years | **12 days** |
| ≥ 5 years | **16 days** |
Pro-rated to completed months if < 12 months' service in the year.

## Sick leave (s.60F)
Outpatient (no hospitalisation), per year:
| Continuous service | Paid outpatient sick leave |
|--------------------|:--------------------------:|
| < 2 years | **14 days** |
| 2 to < 5 years | **18 days** |
| ≥ 5 years | **22 days** |
Plus up to **60 days/year** where **hospitalisation** is necessary (separated from outpatient leave by the 2022 amendment). Certification by a registered medical practitioner / medical officer.

## Public holidays (s.60D)
Minimum **11 paid gazetted public holidays/year**, of which **5 are compulsory**:
1. National Day · 2. Birthday of the Yang di-Pertuan Agong · 3. Birthday of the State Ruler / Yang di-Pertua Negeri (or **Federal Territory Day** if working in a Federal Territory) · 4. Workers' Day (Labour Day) · 5. Malaysia Day.
If a holiday falls on a rest day or another holiday, the next working day substitutes.

## Maternity leave (s.37)
- **98 consecutive days** (raised from 60 by the 2022 amendment, effective 1 Jan 2023).
- **Maternity allowance eligibility:** employed by the employer **≥ 90 days in aggregate during the 9 months** before confinement **and** employed at any time in the **4 months** before confinement.
- No maternity **allowance** if the employee has **5 or more surviving children** at confinement (the leave itself remains).

## Paternity leave (s.60FA — new under the 2022 amendment)
- **7 consecutive days**, paid at the ordinary rate, **per confinement**, limited to **5 confinements**.
- **Eligibility:** married male employee; **same employer ≥ 12 months** immediately before the leave; notified the employer of the spouse's pregnancy ≥ 30 days before expected confinement (or as soon as possible after birth).
