## Summary
For EA-covered employees (effectively those earning **≤ RM4,000/month**, since s.60J is excluded above that), the **Employment (Termination and Lay-Off Benefits) Regulations 1980** set a **minimum termination/lay-off (retrenchment) benefit** of **10 / 15 / 20 days' wages per year of service**, by length of service. This is the statutory severance floor; contracts may provide more.

> Currency: **RM**. Scope: Peninsular Malaysia + Labuan. Employees above RM4,000/month are outside the statutory benefit (s.60J excluded by the EA First Schedule) — their severance is contractual.

## Termination / lay-off (retrenchment) benefit — Regulation 6
| Continuous service | Minimum benefit per year of service |
|--------------------|:-----------------------------------:|
| < 2 years | **10 days' wages** |
| 2 to < 5 years | **15 days' wages** |
| ≥ 5 years | **20 days' wages** |

- Pro-rated for an incomplete year, calculated **to the nearest month**.
- Generally requires **≥ 12 months' continuous service**.
- Applies to EA-covered employees (those earning ≤ RM4,000/month, per the s.60J exclusion above that wage).

> Notice of termination: the minimum notice period is set by the contract or, if silent, by **s.12 of the Employment Act** (by length of service). Retrenchment must follow fair-selection principles; mandatory retrenchment reporting to the Labour Department (Form PK) applies.
