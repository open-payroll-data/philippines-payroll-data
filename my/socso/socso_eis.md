## Summary
**PERKESO** administers two payroll contributions on a **banded RM table capped at a RM6,000 monthly wage ceiling** (raised from RM5,000 effective **1 October 2024**). **SOCSO (Act 4)** — Employment Injury + Invalidity — is roughly **employer ~1.75% + employee 0.5%** for employees under 60 (First Category). **EIS (Act 800)** — Employment Insurance — is **0.2% employer + 0.2% employee**. Both are read from PERKESO's fixed RM bands, not applied as exact percentages.

> Currency: **RM**. Administrator: **PERKESO** (Pertubuhan Keselamatan Sosial / Social Security Organisation). Store and apply the **RM band tables** as the golden source — the percentages are approximations at the ceiling.

## SOCSO (Act 4) — Employment Injury + Invalidity
| Category | Who | Employer | Employee |
|----------|-----|:--------:|:--------:|
| **First Category** | Employees **below 60** | **~1.75%** (≈ RM104.15 at the ceiling) | **0.5%** (≈ RM29.75 at the ceiling) |
| **Second Category** | Employees **aged 60+** (Employment Injury only) | **~1.25%** (≈ RM74.40 at the ceiling) | 0 (employee portion = SKBBK component only) |

- **Wage ceiling: RM6,000/month** (from 1 Oct 2024; was RM5,000). Read the exact contribution from the PERKESO **Act 4 band table**.
- First Category covers both the Employment Injury Scheme and the Invalidity Scheme; employees aged 60+ are outside Invalidity, so only the (employer-borne) Employment Injury applies (Second Category).

## EIS (Act 800) — Employment Insurance System
| | Employer | Employee |
|--|:--------:|:--------:|
| EIS contribution | **0.2%** (RM11.90 at the ceiling) | **0.2%** (RM11.90 at the ceiling) |

- **Wage ceiling: RM6,000/month** (from 1 Oct 2024). Coverage age **18–60**.
- Read the exact contribution from the PERKESO **Act 800 band table**.

## Quick example (employee under 60, wage RM6,000+)
At/above the RM6,000 ceiling: **SOCSO** employer RM104.15 + employee RM29.75; **EIS** employer RM11.90 + employee RM11.90. Below the ceiling, use the PERKESO band tables for the exact RM amounts.
