## Summary
Malaysia's national minimum wage is **RM1,700/month** (hourly **RM8.72**), set by the **Minimum Wages Order 2024 (P.U.(A) 376)**. It took effect **1 February 2025** for employers with **5 or more employees** (and for professional-activity employers of any size); employers with **fewer than 5 employees** paid **RM1,500** until **31 July 2025** and **RM1,700 from 1 August 2025**. So **RM1,700 is universal from 1 Aug 2025**.

> Currency: **RM**. Scope: Peninsular Malaysia, Sabah, Sarawak & Labuan (the Order is national). Excludes **domestic servants**.

## The rate
| Monthly | Hourly | Daily (6-day week) | Daily (5-day) | Daily (4-day) |
|---------|--------|--------------------|----------------|----------------|
| **RM1,700** | **RM8.72** | RM65.38 | RM78.46 | RM98.08 |

### Commencement & small-employer deferral
- **From 1 Feb 2025 (RM1,700):** employers with **≥ 5 employees**, and any employer carrying on a **professional activity** (MASCO classification), regardless of headcount.
- **Employers with < 5 employees** (other than professional-activity employers): **RM1,500/month** from 1 Feb 2025 to 31 Jul 2025, then **RM1,700 from 1 Aug 2025**.
- **Excluded:** domestic servants.

It **revokes** the prior Minimum Wages Order 2022 (P.U.(A) 140/2022, which set RM1,500).
