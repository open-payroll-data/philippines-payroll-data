## Summary
The **HRD Corp levy** (HRDF) is an **employer-only** monthly training levy under the **PSMB Act 2001**. Registered employers with **10 or more** Malaysian employees pay **1%** of each employee's monthly wages (mandatory); employers with **5–9** employees may opt in at **0.5%**. It funds employee training claims and is **not deducted from the employee**.

> Currency: **RM**. Administrator: **HRD Corp** (Human Resource Development Corporation / Pembangunan Sumber Manusia Berhad).

## The levy
| Employer | Rate | Basis |
|----------|:----:|-------|
| **10 or more** Malaysian employees | **1%** (mandatory) | of monthly wages |
| **5–9** Malaysian employees | **0.5%** (optional registration) | of monthly wages |

- **Formula:** `levy = (basic salary − unpaid leave + fixed cash allowances) × rate`.
- **Basis = basic salary + fixed cash allowances**; excludes bonuses, overtime, and non-fixed allowances.
- **Employer-borne** — not deducted from the employee's pay.
