## Summary
For 2025, PhilHealth premium rate is **5%** of monthly basic salary, split equally: **2.5% employee** and **2.5% employer**. Monthly basic salary is capped at **₱100,000** for premium computation. Minimum floor is **₱10,000 MSB** (minimum premium = ₱500 total, ₱250 each side).

> **Golden source:** confirmed directly from PhilHealth **Advisory No. 2025-0002** — 5.0% effective January 2025, income floor ₱10,000, ceiling ₱100,000, citing RA 11223 §10 and PhilHealth Circular No. 2020-0005. **5% is the final scheduled UHC rate** and continues into 2026 (PhilHealth confirmed 5% for CY 2026).

## 2025 Rate at a Glance

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| Premium rate | 5% of monthly basic salary |
| Employee share | 2.5% |
| Employer share | 2.5% |
| Salary floor (MSB) | ₱10,000 |
| Salary ceiling (MSB) | ₱100,000 |
| Minimum monthly premium (total) | ₱500 (₱250 employee + ₱250 employer) |
| Maximum monthly premium (total) | ₱5,000 (₱2,500 employee + ₱2,500 employer) |

---

## Computation Formula

```
Monthly Premium = Monthly Basic Salary × 5%
Employee deduction = Monthly Basic Salary × 2.5%
Employer share = Monthly Basic Salary × 2.5%
```

If monthly basic salary < ₱10,000 → use ₱10,000 as the floor
If monthly basic salary > ₱100,000 → use ₱100,000 as the ceiling

## What is "monthly basic salary"

Monthly basic salary for PhilHealth = the fixed basic pay, **excluding**:
- Overtime pay
- Holiday premium pay
- Night differential pay
- Allowances (transportation, representation, etc.)
- Bonuses and incentives
- 13th month pay

Use only the regular fixed basic pay. Commission earners: use the guaranteed basic pay component.

## Rate history (RA 11223 schedule)

| Year | Scheduled Rate | Actual/Status |
|------|--------------|---------------|
| 2019 | 2.75% | Implemented |
| 2020 | 3.00% | Implemented |
| 2021 | 3.50% | Deferred/suspended |
| 2022 | 4.00% | Implemented (partially) |
| 2023 | 4.50% | Implemented |
| 2024 | 5.00% | Deferred to 2025 per Circular |
| 2025 | 5.00% | **Current** |

The increase to 5% was deferred from 2024 to 2025. **5% is the final tranche of the RA 11223 schedule — there is no higher scheduled rate, so it stays 5% unless a new PhilHealth circular supersedes it** (none as of `last_verified`). The rate here is authoritative for ordinary use — no open-web check needed; for monitoring, watch the PhilHealth advisories index (in *Source & Notes*) each December.

## Remittance schedule

| Employer type | Deadline | Channel |
|--------------|----------|---------|
| All employers | On or before the 12th of the month following the applicable month | PhilHealth-accredited collecting banks, PhilHealth website (EPRS), or payment partners |

Example: January contributions must be remitted on or before February 12.
