## ✅ Provenance (government-confirmed)
The figures below are confirmed from a **government source**: DBM **Circular Letter No. 2024-2** (1 Feb 2024) reproduces **Pag-IBIG Fund Circular No. 460** (dated 15 Jan 2024) — Section C's contribution-rate table and the **Maximum Fund Salary increase from ₱5,000 → ₱10,000** (max ₱200 per side), **effective February 2024**. The HDMF-hosted original of Circular 460 sits behind a reCAPTCHA, but its contents are corroborated verbatim by the DBM circular, so this file is `source_tier: primary`.

---

## Summary
Pag-IBIG (HDMF) contributions are **2% employee + 2% employer** of monthly compensation (employees earning ≤ ₱1,500 pay 1%). Since **February 2024 (Circular 460)** the **Maximum Fund Salary (MFS) is ₱10,000** (raised from ₱5,000), so the **maximum mandatory contribution is ₱200 per side** (total ₱400) — up from the old ₱100/₱100. Employees may contribute more voluntarily.

> **Correction vs earlier draft:** this file previously listed the old ₱5,000 cap / ₱100 max — those are pre-2024 figures and are wrong for 2024 onward. Current cap is ₱10,000 / ₱200 each.

## Mandatory Contribution Table (MFS ₱10,000, since Feb 2024)

| Monthly Compensation | Employee Rate | Employer Rate | Max each | Max total |
|---------------------|--------------|--------------|----------|-----------|
| ₱1,500 and below | 1% | 2% | — | — |
| Over ₱1,500 | 2% | 2% | ₱200 (at ₱10,000 MFS) | ₱400 |

### Formula
```
Monthly comp ≤ ₱1,500:
  Employee = comp × 1%
  Employer = comp × 2%

Monthly comp > ₱1,500:
  Employee = min(comp, ₱10,000) × 2%   → max ₱200
  Employer = min(comp, ₱10,000) × 2%   → max ₱200
```

## Examples

| Monthly Salary | Employee | Employer | Total |
|---------------|----------|----------|-------|
| ₱1,000 | ₱10.00 (1%) | ₱20.00 (2%) | ₱30.00 |
| ₱1,500 | ₱15.00 (1%) | ₱30.00 (2%) | ₱45.00 |
| ₱5,000 | ₱100.00 | ₱100.00 | ₱200.00 |
| ₱10,000 | ₱200.00 | ₱200.00 | ₱400.00 |
| ₱20,000 | ₱200.00 (capped) | ₱200.00 (capped) | ₱400.00 |
| ₱50,000 | ₱200.00 (capped) | ₱200.00 (capped) | ₱400.00 |

The MFS cap of ₱10,000 means salary above ₱10,000 does not increase the mandatory amount.

---

## Voluntary contributions
Employees may contribute **above ₱200/month** (e.g. via MP2 savings or higher regular savings). The employer is not required to match the excess. Higher savings earn more in annual HDMF dividends.

---

## Who is covered (mandatory)
All employees regardless of tenure: private-sector, government (separate from GSIS), kasambahay (employer pays both shares below threshold). OFWs and self-employed may join voluntarily.
