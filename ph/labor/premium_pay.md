## Summary
Philippine premium pay is set by the **Labor Code (PD 442) Arts. 86–94** and consolidated in the **DOLE Handbook on Workers' Statutory Monetary Benefits**. Overtime adds **+25%** on an ordinary day (**+30%** on a rest day, special day, or holiday); **night-shift differential** is **+10%** for work between **10 p.m. and 6 a.m.**; a **regular holiday worked** pays **200%** (not worked, **100%**); a **special non-working day worked** pays **130%**. These are statutory **minimums** — a CBA or company policy may exceed them. They are unchanged 2022–2026.

## Combined multiplier matrix (DOLE Handbook "Guide Computations")

Percentages are of the basic daily/hourly wage. Hourly rate = daily rate ÷ 8.

| Day type | Regular (8 hrs) | + Night shift (×1.10) | + Overtime | + Night shift **and** OT |
|----------|:---------------:|:---------------------:|:----------:|:------------------------:|
| **Ordinary day** | 100% | 110% | **125%** | 137.5% |
| **Rest day** | 130% | 143% | 169% | 185.9% |
| **Special (non-working) day** | 130% | 143% | 169% | 185.9% |
| **Special day on a rest day** | 150% | 165% | 195% | 214.5% |
| **Regular holiday** | **200%** | 220% | 260% | 286% |
| **Regular holiday on a rest day** | 260% | 286% | 338% | 371.8% |
| **Double holiday** (two regular holidays, same day) | **300%** | 330% | 390% | 429% |
| **Double holiday on a rest day** | 390% | 429% | 507% | 557.7% |

OT column = day factor × **1.25** (ordinary day) or × **1.30** (any premium day). Night shift = × **1.10**. The +30% OT premium on a premium day is applied to the *already-premium* hourly rate (the Handbook's multiplicative method).

### Worked example

Basic daily rate **₱1,000** (= ₱125/hour). The employee works **8 hours on a regular holiday that also falls on their rest day**:
- Multiplier = **260%** → day's pay = ₱1,000 × 2.60 = **₱2,600.00**.
- Each **overtime** hour beyond 8 that day = ₱125 × **3.38** = ₱422.50; if that OT hour is also **night-shift** (10 p.m.–6 a.m.) = ₱125 × **3.718** = **₱464.75**.
- If the holiday is **not worked** (and the employee qualifies), they still receive **100%** = ₱1,000.

## The rules, by article

### Overtime — Art. 87
- Ordinary day: **+25%** of hourly rate → **125%**.
- Rest day / special day / holiday: **+30%** of the hourly rate **on that day** → e.g. rest-day OT = 130% × 1.30 = **169%**; regular-holiday OT = 200% × 1.30 = **260%**.

### Night-shift differential — Art. 86
- **+10%** of the regular wage for each hour worked **10:00 p.m.–6:00 a.m.** Stacks (multiplicatively) on the day's premium factor.

### Rest day & special non-working day — Arts. 91–93
- Rest day worked (first 8 hrs): **+30%** → **130%**.
- Special non-working day worked: **+30%** → **130%**; if also a rest day: **+50%** → **150%**.
- Special **working** day: paid as an ordinary day (**100%**). Special non-working day **not worked**: "no work, no pay" unless a CBA/policy says otherwise.

### Holiday pay — Art. 94
- Regular holiday **not worked** (and qualified): **100%** of the daily wage.
- Regular holiday **worked** (first 8 hrs): **200%**.
- **Double holiday** (two regular holidays on the same date) worked: **300%** (200% + 100%); not worked: **200%**.
- **Qualifier:** the employee must be present or on paid leave on the workday **immediately preceding** the regular holiday.

## Who is covered vs. EXEMPT (Art. 82 / Handbook)

OT, NSD, premium pay, and holiday pay all **exclude** the same core groups:

1. **Government** employees (incl. GOCCs with original charters)
2. **Managerial employees** (manage as primary duty; direct ≥2 staff; hire/fire authority)
3. **Officers/members of the managerial staff** (discretion/independent judgment; ≤20% non-managerial work)
4. **Field personnel** (work away from the office; hours not determinable with certainty)
5. **Kasambahay** / persons in the personal service of another
6. **Workers paid by results** (piece-rate, *pakyaw*, *takay*) at DOLE-compliant rates
7. **Family members** dependent on the employer
8. **Holiday pay only:** retail/service establishments with **< 10 workers** are exempt. **NSD only:** retail/service with **≤ 5 workers** are exempt. *(Different thresholds — don't conflate.)*

## Computation base — monthly-paid conversion (EEMR)

**Estimated Equivalent Monthly Rate = (Applicable Daily Rate × Factor) ÷ 12.** To get the daily rate from a monthly salary, invert: **ADR = EEMR × 12 ÷ Factor.**

| Employee type | Factor |
|---------------|:------:|
| Monthly-paid (paid all days incl. rest days & holidays) | **365** |
| Daily-paid, works every day incl. rest/holidays | **393.5** (older DOLE Handbook editions: 392.5) |
| Daily-paid, unpaid on rest days | **313** (or **305** if the 8 special days are unpaid) |
| Daily-paid, unpaid Sat + Sun | **261** (or **253** if special days unpaid) |

Factors are DOLE "suggestions/guides," subject to company policy/CBA.
