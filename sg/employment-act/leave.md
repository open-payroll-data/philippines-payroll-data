## Summary
Singapore's statutory leave is set by the **Employment Act** (annual, sick, public holidays) and the government-paid parental-leave schemes administered by **MOM**. EA-covered employees with ≥3 months' service get **7→14 days** annual leave (by years of service), up to **14 days outpatient / 60 days hospitalisation** sick leave, and **11 paid public holidays**. Parental leave is **actively changing**: Government-Paid **Paternity Leave doubled to 4 weeks** and a new **Shared Parental Leave** began, both keyed to **child's date of birth from 1 Apr 2025**; SPL phases from 6 → 10 weeks for births from 1 Apr 2026.

> **Date everything by the child's date of birth (DOB).** 1 Apr 2025 and 1 Apr 2026 are hard cutovers for paternity and shared parental leave.

## Annual leave (MOM, last updated 17 Apr 2023)
Eligibility: EA-covered, worked **≥3 months**.

| Year of service | Paid annual leave (days) |
|-----------------|--------------------------|
| 1st | 7 |
| 2nd | 8 |
| 3rd | 9 |
| 4th | 10 |
| 5th | 11 |
| 6th | 12 |
| 7th | 13 |
| 8th and thereafter | 14 |

## Public holidays (MOM, last updated 30 Jul 2025 / list 19 Jun 2026)
**11 paid public holidays** a year.
- Required to work on a PH → extra day's salary at the **basic rate of pay** (on top of the gross holiday pay), or a day off / time off in lieu by mutual agreement.
- PH falls on a **rest day** → the next working day becomes the paid PH.
- PH falls on a **non-working day** (e.g. Saturday for a 5-day week) → a day off or salary in lieu.
- Managers/executives (outside Part IV) → time off in lieu (≤4 hrs worked = 4 hrs off; >4 hrs = full day off).

## Sick leave (MOM, last updated 12 Jan 2026)
Eligibility: worked **≥3 months**; notify employer within 48 hours; produce a medical certificate.

| Months of service | Paid outpatient days | Paid hospitalisation days |
|-------------------|----------------------|---------------------------|
| 3 | 5 | 15 |
| 4 | 8 | 30 |
| 5 | 11 | 45 |
| 6 or more | 14 | 60 |

(The 60-day hospitalisation cap is **inclusive** of the 14 outpatient days.) The schedule steps up per MOM's table — outpatient **+3 days** and hospitalisation **+15 days** per month of service from month 3 to month 6 — so the uneven outpatient:hospitalisation ratio across rows is the actual MOM schedule, not a transcription error.

## Parental leave ⚠ 2025 reforms — key by child's DOB

### Government-Paid Maternity Leave (GPML) — MOM, last updated 5 Jan 2026
- **16 weeks** if the child is a SG citizen and the mother has ≥3 months' service. (If the child is not a SG citizen: **12 weeks** under the EA, last 4 weeks unpaid.)
- **Who pays (1st/2nd child):** employer pays the first 8 weeks; government reimburses the last 8 (cap **$10,000 per 4 weeks / $20,000 total**). **3rd+ child:** government reimburses all 16 weeks (cap $10,000 per 4 weeks / **$40,000** total).

### Government-Paid Paternity Leave (GPPL) — MOM, last updated 5 Feb 2026
- Child born **before 1 Apr 2025:** 2 weeks mandatory + 2 weeks voluntary.
- Child born **on/after 1 Apr 2025:** **4 weeks** (doubled), **mandatory** — employer cannot refuse.
- Eligibility: child is SG citizen; father lawfully married to the mother; ≥3 months' service. Employer pays, reimbursed for all 4 weeks (cap **$2,500/week / $10,000 total**).

### Shared Parental Leave (SPL) — NEW scheme — MOM, last updated 10 Feb 2026
Shared between the parents, taken within 12 months of birth:

| Child's DOB | SPL entitlement |
|-------------|-----------------|
| 1 Apr 2025 – 31 Mar 2026 | **6 weeks** |
| On/after 1 Apr 2026 | **10 weeks** |

Eligibility mirrors GPPL; employer pays, reimbursed (cap **$2,500/week**). This new SPL **replaces** the old (2013) shared-parental-leave scheme; births 1 Jul 2017 – 31 Mar 2025 stay under the older scheme.

### Childcare leave — MOM, last updated 24 Jul 2025
| Type | Days/yr | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Government-Paid Childcare Leave | **6** | Youngest child < 7, SG citizen, ≥3 months' service. Employer pays first 3 days; govt reimburses last 3 (cap $500/day incl. CPF). Lifetime cap 42 days/child. |
| EA Childcare Leave (non-citizen child) | **2** | Child < 7, ≥3 months' service. |
| Extended Childcare Leave | **2** | Child aged 7–12. |

### Adoption Leave (GPAL) — MOM, last updated 5 Jan 2026
**12 weeks** for the adoptive mother (adopted child < 12 months at formal intent to adopt; SG citizen; ≥3 months' service). Employer pays first 4 weeks; govt reimburses remaining 8 (1st/2nd: cap $10,000/4 wks, $20,000 total; 3rd+: all 12 weeks, $30,000 cap).

### Unpaid Infant Care Leave (UICL) — MOM, last updated 15 May 2024
**12 days per parent per year** (raised from 6 on 1 Jan 2024). Child < 2 in the 12-month period; SG citizen; ≥3 months' service.
