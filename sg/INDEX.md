## Key authorities & instruments

| Authority / Instrument | What it covers |
|------------------------|----------------|
| IRAS (Income Tax Act) | Personal income tax, employer reporting (IR8A/AIS), tax clearance (IR21) |
| CPF Board (CPF Act) | CPF contributions; collects the Skills Development Levy |
| MOM (Employment Act) | Coverage, hours/overtime (Part IV), leave, termination, retrenchment |
| MOM (PWM / LQS) | Sector wage floors and the Local Qualifying Salary |
