## Summary
Vietnam taxes resident employment income on a **7-bracket progressive scale (5%–35%)**, on a **monthly** basis (top rate **35%** above **80,000,000 VND/month**). Each resident gets a **personal deduction** — **⚠ raised from 1 January 2026** to **15,500,000 VND/month** for the taxpayer and **6,200,000 VND/month per dependent** (was 11M / 4.4M through 2025). Compulsory insurance contributions are deducted **before** tax. **Non-residents** pay a flat **20%** on Vietnam-source employment income. Employers **withhold monthly** (PAYE) for employees with a labour contract of **≥ 3 months**; payments to others ≥ 2,000,000 are withheld at a flat **10%**.

> Currency: Vietnamese Dong (**VND**). Tax authority: **General Department of Taxation (GDT)**.

## Resident PIT — 7-bracket monthly scale (Art. 22)
Applied to **assessable income** = gross employment income − compulsory insurance − personal & dependent deductions.

| Monthly assessable income (VND) | Rate |
|---------------------------------|:----:|
| Up to 5,000,000 | 5% |
| Over 5,000,000 to 10,000,000 | 10% |
| Over 10,000,000 to 18,000,000 | 15% |
| Over 18,000,000 to 32,000,000 | 20% |
| Over 32,000,000 to 52,000,000 | 25% |
| Over 52,000,000 to 80,000,000 | 30% |
| Over 80,000,000 | **35%** |

*(Annual brackets = monthly × 12: 60M / 120M / 216M / 384M / 624M / 960M thresholds.)*

## Personal deductions (giảm trừ gia cảnh)
| Period | Taxpayer (self) | Per dependent |
|--------|-----------------|---------------|
| Through 31 Dec 2025 (incl. the 2025 finalisation) | 11,000,000/month | 4,400,000/month |
| **From 1 Jan 2026 (tax period 2026 onward)** | **15,500,000/month** | **6,200,000/month** |

Order of computation: **gross − compulsory insurance (8% SI + 1.5% HI + 1% UI) − personal/dependent deductions = assessable income → apply the table.**

## Non-resident employment income
- Flat **20%** on Vietnam-source salary/wage income (no deductions, no progressive scale).
- **Resident** if present in Vietnam **≥ 183 days** in a calendar year or in 12 consecutive months from arrival, **or** has a registered/leased permanent residence. Otherwise non-resident.

## Monthly withholding (PAYE)
- **Labour contract ≥ 3 months:** employer withholds monthly on the progressive scale (after insurance + deductions).
- **No contract / contract < 3 months:** withhold a flat **10%** on each payment of **≥ 2,000,000 VND** (provisional; reconciled at annual finalisation; a commitment form can exempt low-income individuals).
- **Annual finalisation (quyết toán):** residents reconcile total liability vs. amounts withheld (via employer or self).

## Quick example (resident, 2026; assessable income 25,000,000/month)
Tax = 5%×5M + 10%×5M + 15%×8M + 20%×(25M−18M) = 250,000 + 500,000 + 1,200,000 + 1,400,000 = **3,350,000 VND**. *(Assessable income is already net of insurance and the 15.5M/6.2M deductions.)*
