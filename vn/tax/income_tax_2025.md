## Summary
Vietnam's resident employment-income tax scale changed for **2026**: the new **Law 109/2025/QH15** (passed 10 Dec 2025) replaces the old 7-bracket scale with a **5-bracket progressive scale (5%–35%)** on a **monthly** basis — top rate **35%** now applies above **100,000,000 VND/month** (up from 80M). Each resident gets a **personal deduction** of **15,500,000 VND/month** (self) + **6,200,000 VND/month per dependent** (raised for 2026). Compulsory insurance is deducted **before** tax. **Non-residents** pay a flat **20%** on Vietnam-source employment income. Employers **withhold monthly** for contracts **≥ 3 months**; other payments ≥ 2,000,000 are withheld at a flat **10%**.

> Currency: Vietnamese Dong (**VND**). Tax authority: **General Department of Taxation (GDT)**.

> **Timing (Law 109/2025/QH15, Điều 29):** the law's general effective date is **1 July 2026**, **but** the provisions on **resident salary/wage and business income apply from the full 2026 tax period** (all of calendar 2026). So the **5-bracket table below governs 2026 payroll/finalisation**; the old 7-bracket scale applies through the **2025** tax period.

## Resident PIT — 5-bracket monthly scale (Law 109/2025/QH15, from the 2026 tax period)
Applied to **assessable income** = gross employment income − compulsory insurance − personal & dependent deductions.

| Monthly assessable income (VND) | Annual (VND) | Rate |
|---------------------------------|--------------|:----:|
| Up to 10,000,000 | Up to 120,000,000 | 5% |
| Over 10,000,000 to 30,000,000 | 120M–360M | 10% |
| Over 30,000,000 to 60,000,000 | 360M–720M | 20% |
| Over 60,000,000 to 100,000,000 | 720M–1,200M | 30% |
| Over 100,000,000 | Over 1,200,000,000 | **35%** |

### Historical: 7-bracket scale (Law 04/2007/QH12 — through the 2025 tax period)
5% ≤5M; 10% 5–10M; 15% 10–18M; 20% 18–32M; 25% 32–52M; 30% 52–80M; 35% >80M (monthly). **Use this for the 2025 finalisation; use the 5-bracket table above for 2026 onward.**

## Personal deductions (giảm trừ gia cảnh)
| Period | Taxpayer (self) | Per dependent |
|--------|-----------------|---------------|
| Through the 2025 tax period | 11,000,000/month | 4,400,000/month |
| **From the 2026 tax period** | **15,500,000/month** | **6,200,000/month** |

(The 15.5M/6.2M amounts were first set by Resolution 110/2025/UBTVQH15 and are restated in Law 109/2025/QH15 Điều 10.) Order: **gross − compulsory insurance (8% SI + 1.5% HI + 1% UI) − personal/dependent deductions = assessable income → apply the table.**

## Non-resident employment income
- Flat **20%** on Vietnam-source salary/wage income (no deductions, no progressive scale).
- **Resident** if present in Vietnam **≥ 183 days** in a calendar year or in 12 consecutive months from arrival, **or** has a registered/leased permanent residence. Otherwise non-resident.

## Monthly withholding (PAYE) — Circular 111/2013 Art. 25
- **Labour contract ≥ 3 months:** employer withholds monthly on the progressive scale (after insurance + deductions).
- **No contract / < 3 months:** withhold a flat **10%** on each payment **≥ 2,000,000 VND** (provisional; reconciled at annual finalisation; a commitment form can exempt low-income individuals).
- **Annual finalisation (quyết toán):** residents reconcile total liability vs. amounts withheld.

## Quick example (resident, 2026; assessable income 25,000,000/month)
Under the new 5-bracket scale: tax = 5%×10,000,000 + 10%×(25,000,000−10,000,000) = 500,000 + 1,500,000 = **2,000,000 VND**. *(Assessable income is already net of insurance and the 15.5M/6.2M deductions. Under the old 7-bracket scale this would have been 3,350,000 — the new scale is lower for mid earners.)*
