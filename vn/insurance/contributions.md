## Summary
Vietnam has **three compulsory insurances**. Totals: **employee 10.5%**, **employer 21.5%** of the contribution wage. Split: **Social Insurance (BHXH)** employee **8%** / employer **17.5%** (the employer share includes 0.5% for the occupational-accident/disease fund); **Health Insurance (BHYT)** **1.5% / 3%**; **Unemployment Insurance (BHTN)** **1% / 1%**. **SI and HI** are capped at **20× the reference level** (= the base salary **2,340,000 VND**, so cap = **46,800,000 VND/month**); **UI** is capped at **20× the regional minimum wage**. **Foreign employees** pay SI + HI but **not** unemployment insurance.

> Currency: **VND**. Administrator: **Vietnam Social Security (VSS / BHXH Việt Nam)**.

## Contribution rates
| Insurance | Employee | Employer | Contribution-wage cap |
|-----------|:--------:|:--------:|-----------------------|
| **Social Insurance (BHXH)** | **8%** | **17.5%** (incl. 0.5% occupational accident/disease) | 20× reference level = **46,800,000/mo** |
| **Health Insurance (BHYT)** | **1.5%** | **3%** | 20× reference level = **46,800,000/mo** |
| **Unemployment Insurance (BHTN)** | **1%** | **1%** | 20× regional minimum wage |
| **Total** | **10.5%** | **21.5%** | — |

- **Reference level (mức tham chiếu) = 2,340,000 VND/month** (currently equal to the former base salary; from 1 Jul 2024 per Decree 73/2024).
- **Contribution base** = the labour-contract salary + wage allowances (phụ cấp lương) + other fixed regular payments. Floor = the reference level; SI/HI ceiling = 46,800,000.

## Foreign employees
Subject to **SI (8% / 17.5%)** and **HI (1.5% / 3%)** but **not** unemployment insurance → **employee 9.5% / employer 20.5%**.

---

## Quick example (local employee, contribution wage 20,000,000/month)
- Below the 46.8M cap → on the full 20,000,000.
- Employee: SI 1,600,000 (8%) + HI 300,000 (1.5%) + UI 200,000 (1%) = **2,100,000 (10.5%)**.
- Employer: SI 3,500,000 (17.5%) + HI 600,000 (3%) + UI 200,000 (1%) = **4,300,000 (21.5%)**.
