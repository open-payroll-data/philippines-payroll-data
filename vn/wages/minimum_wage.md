## Summary
Vietnam sets a **monthly and hourly minimum wage across 4 regions** (Region I = major urban areas incl. Hanoi & HCMC → Region IV = least-developed rural), by government decree. **⚠ The current decree is No. 293/2025/ND-CP, effective 1 January 2026** (it replaced Decree 74/2024). Region I = **5,310,000 VND/month** (hourly 25,500), down to Region IV = **3,700,000/month** (hourly 17,800) — an average ~7.2% rise. The regional minimum wage is also the **20× cap reference** for unemployment-insurance contributions.

> Currency: **VND**. Set by government decree on the recommendation of the National Wage Council.

> `volatility: continuously-changing` — revised by decree roughly annually (effective 1 Jan or 1 Jul). **Re-pull the latest decree before relying on a figure.** Decree 293/2025 (1 Jan 2026) superseded Decree 74/2024 (1 Jul 2024).

## Regional minimum wage — Decree 293/2025/ND-CP (from 1 Jan 2026)
| Region | Monthly (VND) | Hourly (VND) | Covers |
|--------|--------------:|-------------:|--------|
| **I** | **5,310,000** | 25,500 | Major urban (Hanoi, HCMC, Hai Phong, Da Nang core districts) |
| **II** | **4,730,000** | 22,700 | Smaller cities / suburban districts |
| **III** | **4,140,000** | 20,000 | Provincial towns / some districts |
| **IV** | **3,700,000** | 17,800 | Remaining (rural) localities |

Region assignment is by the district/commune list annexed to the decree.

### Prior decree (superseded — for history)
Decree 74/2024/ND-CP (1 Jul 2024 – 31 Dec 2025): Region I 4,960,000 / II 4,410,000 / III 3,860,000 / IV 3,450,000 (hourly 23,800 / 21,200 / 18,600 / 16,600).

> **Unemployment-insurance cap:** UI contributions are capped at **20× the regional minimum wage** — so the cap rose on 1 Jan 2026 with this decree (Region I: 20 × 5,310,000 = 106,200,000/month).
