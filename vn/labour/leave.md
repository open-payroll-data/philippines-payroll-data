## Summary
Under the **Labour Code 2019**, employees get **12 working days** of paid annual leave (14 for arduous/hazardous work or minors, 16 for especially arduous), **+1 day per 5 years** with the same employer, and **11 paid public holidays** per year. **Maternity leave is 6 months** (paid by Social Insurance at **100%** of the average insured salary); **paternity leave is 5–14 days** (via Social Insurance).

> Currency: **VND**. Statutes: **Labour Code 2019** + the **Law on Social Insurance**.

## Annual leave (Art. 113–114)
| Condition | Paid annual leave/year |
|-----------|:----------------------:|
| Normal working conditions | **12 days** |
| Minors / disabled / arduous, toxic or dangerous work | 14 days |
| Especially arduous, toxic or dangerous work | 16 days |
**+1 day for every 5 years** of service with the same employer (Art. 114).

## Public holidays (Art. 112) — 11 days
| Holiday | Days |
|---------|:----:|
| Gregorian New Year (1 Jan) | 1 |
| Lunar New Year (Tết) | 5 |
| Hung Kings Commemoration (10th day, 3rd lunar month) | 1 |
| Reunification/Victory Day (30 Apr) | 1 |
| International Labour Day (1 May) | 1 |
| National Day (2 Sep + an adjacent day) | 2 |
| **Total** | **11** |
(Foreign employees also get +1 day for their home country's traditional New Year and +1 for its National Day.)

## Maternity & paternity
- **Maternity: 6 months** of leave (prenatal ≤ 2 months), +1 month per additional child in a multiple birth; benefit = **100% of the average of the 6 months' insured salary**, **paid by Social Insurance** (not the employer).
- **Paternity (via SI):** **5 days** (normal); **7** (caesarean or birth before 32 weeks); **10** (twins; +3 per additional child); **14** (twins+ by caesarean). Taken within the first 30 days after birth (60 days under the 2024 SI Law).
