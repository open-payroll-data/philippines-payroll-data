## Summary
On termination, two distinct allowances may apply under the **Labour Code 2019**: **severance allowance** (*trợ cấp thôi việc*, Art. 46) = **½ month's wage per year of service** for an employee with ≥ 12 months' service; and **job-loss/redundancy allowance** (*trợ cấp mất việc làm*, Art. 47) = **1 month's wage per year of service (minimum 2 months)** on economic/restructuring terminations. For **both**, the years counted **exclude any period covered by unemployment insurance** — and since UI has been compulsory in Vietnam from **2009**, in practice the employer-paid allowance typically covers only pre-2009 (or otherwise UI-uncovered) service, with the UI fund covering the rest.

> Currency: **VND**. Statute: **Labour Code 2019, Art. 46–47**. Wage base = the **average of the last 6 months'** contract salary.

## Severance allowance — Art. 46 (*trợ cấp thôi việc*)
- Payable to an employee who worked **≥ 12 months** on termination (except those entitled to a pension or dismissed for disciplinary cause).
- **= ½ month's wage per year of service.**
- Qualifying years = total actual service **minus** the period covered by unemployment insurance (and any period already paid severance/redundancy).
- Wage = average of the **last 6 months'** contract salary.

## Job-loss / redundancy allowance — Art. 47 (*trợ cấp mất việc làm*)
- Payable on termination for **economic / restructuring / merger** reasons (Art. 34(11)).
- **= 1 month's wage per year of service**, **minimum 2 months' wage**.
- Same UI-covered-years exclusion and 6-month-average wage base as Art. 46.
