## Summary
Under the **Labour Code 2019**, normal working time is **≤ 8 hours/day and ≤ 48 hours/week** (the State *encourages* a 40-hour week). **Overtime** is paid at **≥ 150%** on a normal day, **≥ 200%** on a weekly rest day, and **≥ 300%** on a public holiday/paid-leave day (on top of the holiday pay itself); **night work** adds **≥ 30%**, and **overtime at night** adds a further **≥ 20%**. Overtime is capped at **≤ 50% of normal hours/day, ≤ 40 hours/month, and ≤ 200 hours/year** (up to **300 hours/year** for listed sectors, with written notice to the provincial labour authority).

> Currency: **VND**. Statute: **Labour Code 2019, Law No. 45/2019/QH14** (effective 1 Jan 2021).

## Working hours (Art. 105)
- **≤ 8 hours/day** and **≤ 48 hours/week** (where a weekly basis applies, ≤ 10 hours/day but still ≤ 48 hours/week).
- The State **encourages a 40-hour week**.

## Overtime & night pay (Art. 98)
| Situation | Pay |
|-----------|-----|
| Overtime on a normal day | **≥ 150%** of the normal hourly rate |
| Overtime on a weekly rest day | **≥ 200%** |
| Overtime on a public holiday / paid-leave day | **≥ 300%** (plus the holiday/leave pay itself) |
| Night work (10 p.m.–6 a.m.) | **+ ≥ 30%** of the normal rate |
| Overtime **at night** | a further **+ ≥ 20%** (on top of the above) |

## Overtime limits (Art. 107)
- **≤ 50% of normal hours in a day** (and total ≤ 12 hours/day where weekly basis applies).
- **≤ 40 hours per month.**
- **≤ 200 hours per year** — up to **300 hours/year** for listed sectors (textile/garment/footwear/electronics, agro-forestry-aquaculture processing, salt, power, telecom, water, urgent/seasonal work, etc.), with **written notice to the provincial labour authority**.
